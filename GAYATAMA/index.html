Background Of Decoview

DecoView is an AR (Augmented Reality) based application that helps users design and visualize their rooms in real-time. With the integrated camera feature, users can record their space and instantly add furniture or décor elements virtually. This allows them to see if the items fit and match the room before making a purchase.

DecoView Goals

  • Enhance Design Efficiency

    Provide a practical solution for users to design their spaces quickly and effectively through real-time visualization using Augmented Reality technology.

  • Minimize Purchase Errors

    Help users choose the right furniture and décor by visualizing sizes, colors, and item placement before making a purchase.

  • Deliver an Innovative User Experience

    Integrate advanced features such as AR camera functionality and 3D furniture arrangement to create a modern and enjoyable design experience.
